# Saltwater Fish for Declan

A curated list of ~1,000 saltwater/reef fish for Declan's Digital Aquarium.
Focused on reef hobby fish - the species you'd see at frag swaps, LFS visits, and in fellow hobbyists' tanks.

**Display:** 8 fish per day | **Cycle:** Each fish appears ~3x per year
